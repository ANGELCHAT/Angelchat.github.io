# About AngelChat Platform

**AngelChat** is an online customer service software with live support, help desk and web analytics capabilities. It's used by more than 28,000 companies in over 150 countries. **AngelChat Platform** is an ecosystem for developers that grew around AngelChat products over the years.

From the technical perspective the core of AngelChat Platform is the **open messaging protocol**. The Messaging APIs let you integrate any communication channels directly into AngelChat. From the business point of view, we're a team dedicated to build a vivid [AngelChat Marketplace](https://gitter.im/angelchatio) of apps and services.

We've just scratched the surface. There is much more for you to discover! Go ahead and see the full Platform Overview, or dive deep into Platform Toolkits.
